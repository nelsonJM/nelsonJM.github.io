Project summary

Work - What I did

I studied Intercom's design process framework - presented by Alex Potrivaev - and decided to create an artifact that I could use for future projects as a reference. In the video above, I walk through the artifact.

Output - What I delivered

  • A blank PDF worksheet to be filled out on your next project
  • An example worksheet
  • The Sketch file

Outcome - What that achieved

No need to watch/re-watch Alex's presentation. I have a simple, clear document that I can use to recall the important points and share the framework with others.

Impact - What changed

"Design process" can be a daunting topic. There are so many methods to choose from and it can be tempting to think a one-size-fits-all approach is necessary. This framework makes it easier to tailor your process to the needs of the project by forcing you to ask/answer fundamental questions.
