The Reason This App Exists

Here's the situation that inspired me to build this app: I have to count the attendees in a meeting, so I begin by looking around the room, counting heads and keeping track of the number in my memory. When I'm done, I'll write it down or put it in a note taking app. If someone interrupts me while counting, I sometimes have to start over or ask them to wait. When I'm in a meeting and I need to count attendees, I want to keep track of my count without taking my eyes off of the people I'm counting. This app helps me do that.
