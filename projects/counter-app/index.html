A Counter App for iOS—Coming Soon

The Reason This App Exists: A Situation

I count roughly 90-120 meeting attendees each month. I begin by looking around the room, counting heads and keeping track of the number in my mind. When I'm done, I write down the number or put it in a note taking app. If someone interrupts me while counting, I sometimes have to start over or ask them to wait. When I'm in a meeting and I need to count attendees, I want to keep track of my count without taking my eyes off of the people I'm counting. This app helps me do that.
