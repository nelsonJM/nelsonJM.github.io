The Details (the whole messy story)

Problem Overview

This is a time-tracking app for individuals and teams to track their time spent on projects. This overview is subject to change as the design progresses.

It's not easy to remember to track my time at the outset of a project-related task. I am usually working on several projects throughout my day, switching from task to task and stopping mid-task to resolve the occasional emergency. Ideally, I would say something like "Ok, I'm now going to work on project B, so I'll begin tracking my time", but in reality, I just do my work and record my time later.

I am interested in this part of the problem: tracking time spent on a project after the fact.

The Design Process Story

A couple of points before I get started:

  • I want to share the the messy story I go through to arrive at the final design.
  • I'm interested in "adding time after the fact", but this may change as the problem shifts.

Main Capabilities

The following are capabilities the app will offer:

  • Add time to a project for "today" and any given day.
  • See how much time I spent on a project.
  • See how much time my team has spent on a project.
  • See how I spent my time this month.

After identifying the capabilities, I did the following:

  • Wrote down words and concepts from the domain. Examples are: team and project.
  • Sketched user flows. I began with "How do I add time to a project?".
  • Sketched screens/views
