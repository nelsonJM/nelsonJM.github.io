About Josh

I like taking photos of notes

Like this one:

It's a study plan with a view to becoming an Interactive Designer. A friend and mentor of mine sketched it out for me a few years ago inside a Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf.

I was seeking direction in the wilderness of code tutorials and design literature. This plan showed me a "way" and kept me focused when the territory became overwhelming.

Nowadays, I continue to build upon these skills with new methods, tools, and approaches.

Ultimately, my aim is to understand the life situation surrounding the particular design I am creating. This means identifying motivations, desired outcomes and constraints, to design something that balances those forces and improves the situation.
