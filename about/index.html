About Josh

I'm a self-taught designer who enjoys separating motivation from implementation to help people make progress.

Some of my abilities include:

  • UI design
  • Creating high-fidelity mockups in Sketch and Figma
  • Writing UX copy
  • Sketching storyboards and writing stories to guide design efforts
  • Writing basic vanilla JavaScript
  • Troubleshooting in the browser with Chrome DevTools
  • Prototyping in HTML and CSS

These days I'm especially interested in the intersection of design, consumer behavior, markets, and business strategy.
