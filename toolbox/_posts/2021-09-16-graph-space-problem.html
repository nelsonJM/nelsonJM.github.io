We occupy 3D space.

However, problems can be described by 6 dimensions.

Just like we can explore the room we're sitting in, we can explore a problem space by answering the questions 'who/what?', 'how many?', 'where?', 'when?', 'how?', and 'why?'.

One neat connection

Designing interactions is about answering 'how?'. You get there by answering the preceding questions.
