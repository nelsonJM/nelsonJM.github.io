The 4 layers:

  • Outcome - What are we trying to achieve?
  • System - What are the boundaries of our solution? What other systems will it interact with? What are the parts?
  • Interaction - How will someone move through our system? How will they accomplish things?
  • Visual - What's the layout, typography, color, style?
