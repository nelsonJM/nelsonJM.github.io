Hello,

I'm Josh Nelson, a designer living in Austin, Texas. I enjoy helping customers make progress with design that fits their real-life situation.

Brief Origin Story

My design journey began when a friend advised me to learn UX design, front-end development, graphic design and mobile-first design. He helped me put together a study plan for tackling these areas of design and more.

"Design is..."

What is design? Here's a list of responses I would've given throughout my career:

  • Code, Style & Typography
  • Interaction Design
  • Gestalt & User Experience Design - the 'How?'
  • Separating Motivation from Implementation or Helping People Make Progress - the 'Why?'

Each response reflects my interest at a given point in time. I am and I expect to continue cycling through these themes throughout my career.
