// My Design Process

From a high level, the phases of my design process are:

  • Outcome: what effect am I trying to create?
  • System: what's the system that I will design to create that effect?
  • Interaction: what are the system parts and how will someone interact with them?
  • Visual: what's the arrangement, style, etc. of the elements?
