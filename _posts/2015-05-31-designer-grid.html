I was inspired by this Intercom blog post to map my own knowledge and skills across 4 layers of design: Outcome, System, Interaction, and Visual.

I consider myself competent in 'Visual' and 'Interaction' design as they are defined in the x-axis; most of my work has involved creating websites that valued aesthetics/style/emotion over functional considerations. Although I appreciate 'Visual' design, I've discovered a passion for the work conducted in the other 3 layers.
