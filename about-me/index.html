Hello,

I'm Josh Nelson. I'm a designer living in Austin, Texas. I enjoy helping customers make progress with design that fits their real-life situation.

Brief Origin Story

My design journey began when a friend advised me to learn UX design, front-end development, graphic design and mobile-first design. He helped me put together a study plan for tackling these areas of design and more.

I learned quickly and soon took on freelance work, designing and building websites like www.austinfirewood.com and www.aacmclinic.com.

Using Design to Help People Make Progress

I am at my best when solving real problems experienced by real people with real intentions. This was the motivation for designing Counting Eye. Understanding the customer's viewpoint and how they make progress from one life situation to another and designing something that will do it better is extremely rewarding.
