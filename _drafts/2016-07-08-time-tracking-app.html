Purpose

This is a time-tracking app that I designed some time ago. I have two main reasons for posting here:

  • I want to share the the messy story I went through (as best as I can recall) to get to this point.
  • I'm interested in exploring the design, in particular the "adding time" functionality.

Whether or not I'll try to build the whole thing is still unclear.

Overview of the Design Process

Main Capabilities

The following are capabilities the app will offer:

  • Add time to a project for "today" and any given day.
  • See how much time I spent on a project.
  • See how much time my team has spent on a project.
  • See how I spent my time this month.

After identifying the capabilities, I did the following:

  • Wrote down words and concepts from the domain. Examples are: team and project.
  • Sketched user flows. I began with "How do I add time to a project?".
  • Sketched screens/views
